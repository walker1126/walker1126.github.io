Di Yang 杨棣

Ph.D. Candidate, Inria

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Vision at Inria, STARS team in France, supervised by Dr. François Brémond. Simultaneously, I have an academic-industrial collaboration with Dr. Gianpiero Francesca from Toyota Motor Europe and Dr. Quan Kong from Woven by Toyota. I received my M.Eng. in Computer Vision in 2019 from Télécom Saint-Étienne of University of Lyon, France. I earned my B.S. in Communication Engineering in 2017 from Xidian University, China. My research interests include computer vision and deep learning with special interest in video understanding and action recognition.
